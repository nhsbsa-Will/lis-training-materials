Personal details Do you know your NHS Number?

You don't have to tell us your NHS Number, but telling us helps us to identify you correctly and match your details to your health records.

Your NHS Number is a 10-digit number, like 485 777 3456.

What is your NHS Number?

Your NHS Number is not the same as your National Insurance number, which has letters in it.

What is your NHS Number?
Where to find your NHS number

If you're registered with a GP, you'll have a 10-digit NHS Number that is unique to you.

It's usually on any letter or document you've received from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions, usually underneath your name and address
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes
  • an NHS medical card
